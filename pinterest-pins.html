✦ DON'T PANIC

Your flowers arrive
sleepy. Here's the fix.

When fresh flowers are delivered they look thirsty & limp — that's totally normal. Here's how to wake them up. 🌸
1
Unbox right away — gently unwrap and let them breathe.
2
Snip stems at a 45° angle — about an inch off, with clean shears.
3
Deep drink of cool water — clean buckets, no leaves below the waterline.
4
Rest 2–4 hours in a cool, dark spot — and watch them bloom back to life.
